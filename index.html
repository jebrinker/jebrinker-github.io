About the Artist

Jamie Brinker is born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. She attended highschool at Saint Joseph Academy were she participated in art classes for four years. She is currently attending Cleveland Insititute of Art, majoring in Painting. Along with paint, Jamie works in mixed media, digital media, and collage work. Common themes in her work include surrealism, relationship dynamics, and nostolgia.

Contact Jamie at jebrinker@student.cia.edu
